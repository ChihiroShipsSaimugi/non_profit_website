Who We Are:

Recinos Health Organization (RHO) is a Non-Profit Organization dedicated to the research of several different Cancers.

What We Do:

At Recinos Health Organization, we are dedicated to improving the health and well-being of underserved communities through a comprehensive approach to healthcare. Our mission is to ensure that everyone has access to quality medical services, education, and resources necessary for a healthy lifestyle. Our Programs Include, Community Health Clinics: We operate multiple clinics that provide primary care services, preventive screenings, and health education. Our goal is to make healthcare accessible and affordable for all individuals, regardless of their background. Health Education Initiatives: We believe in empowering individuals with knowledge. Our workshops and seminars cover essential topics such as nutrition, exercise, mental health, and chronic disease management, enabling community members to make informed health choices. Mobile Health Services: To reach those who may not be able to visit our clinics, we offer mobile health units that travel to various neighborhoods. These units provide vaccinations, screenings, and immediate medical care in a convenient and compassionate setting. Mental Health Support: Our organization recognizes the importance of mental health in overall wellness. We offer counseling services, support groups, and resources to help individuals navigate their mental health journeys. At the Recinos Health Organization, we envision a future where every individual has the opportunity to lead a healthy life. Together, we can build stronger, healthier communities.
